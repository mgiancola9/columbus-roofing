Common Questions

How much does a new roof cost in Columbus, OH?

Most Columbus homeowners pay between $5,900 and $12,800 for a full roof replacement, depending on roof size, materials, and the scope of work. Labor typically runs $37–$55 per hour. Get a free estimate to see exact pricing for your home.

Will my insurance cover storm damage?

If your roof was damaged by hail, wind, or a severe storm, your homeowner's insurance will typically cover the repair or replacement minus your deductible. Our contractors can help you document damage and navigate the claims process.

How do I know if I have storm damage?

Signs include missing or cracked shingles, granules in your gutters, dented gutters or downspouts, and water stains on ceilings inside. A free inspection is the best way to know for sure — book one today.

How long does a roof replacement take in Columbus?

Most residential roof replacements in Columbus take 1–3 days depending on the size of the home and weather. Your contractor will provide a specific timeline in your estimate.
